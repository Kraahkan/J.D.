ABOUT ME

My passion is doing awesome
things with awesome people

Creative mind, musician, coffee lover, Christian, hiker, and man of winter. Born and raised among the misty mountains of British Columbia. My passions include making things, building good relationships, and living well.

My picture of daily success is to be present, passionate, and productive. As a creative person, it's tough to balance the drive to work with slowing down and spending time reflecting and being in the moment. I try my hardest to strike a balance, so I can not only do stuff that matters but be present while it happens.

Ultimately, I believe there is nothing more important than people, and love being involved in pursuits that improve our lives.
